Get Started With FlyEasyGO

1. Visit FlyEasyGO Website

  • Open FlyEasyGO on your browser.
  • Use the search bar to enter your destination, check-in & check-out dates.

2. Choose Your Preferred Hotel

  • Browse through the available hotels.
  • Filter options by price, rating, amenities, and location.
  • Click on a hotel to see photos, reviews, and detailed information.

3. Select Room Type

  • Pick the room that fits your needs.
  • Check for special offers, free cancellation, and meal options.

4. Enter Guest Details

  • Fill in your name, contact details, and number of guests.
  • Add any special requests (e.g., late check-in, extra bed).

5. Make Secure Payment

  • Choose your payment method (credit card, debit card, PayPal, etc.).
  • Enter payment details and confirm the booking.

6. Receive Booking Confirmation

  • Get an instant email confirmation with booking details.
  • Save the confirmation for easy check-in at the hotel.

7. Manage Your Booking

  • Modify or cancel your booking via your FlyEasyGO account.
  • Contact customer support for assistance if needed.
